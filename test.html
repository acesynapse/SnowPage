Part-Time Library Page-Children’s Department

The Olean Public Library has a part-time Library Page position open in the Children’s Department. Exemplary customer service and excellent computer skills are required. Must be able to assist with early literacy programs for families, daycares and schools, Experience in an early childhood setting preferred. Able to organize and shelve the collection, create and maintain displays, and assist with other library activities as needed.

Schedule: 

  • 18-23 hours per week (varies)
  • The schedule for this position is variable and requires day, night, and weekend hours dependent upon the events scheduled each week.

Duties:

  • Help customers find library items
  • Assist with children’s programs and special events
  • Read, play and make music with infants and children under 5
  • Clean and straighten play area
  • Shelve items to their proper location
  • Answering phones / signing customers up for programs
  • Other tasks may include folding newsletters, preparing materials for programs, creating bulletin boards

Required:

  • Excellent interpersonal skills and dedication to the programming goals of the library
  • The ability to effectively communicate, engage and work with all age groups, specifically children and their caregivers.
  • The ability to work in a team environment and to handle a fast-paced public service setting.
  • Excellent computer skills. Assist patrons with mobile devices, computer issues, scanning, troubleshoot printers, and Internet searches
  • Ability to push heavy book carts
  • Ability to shelve library materials at ground level and up high (kneeling and using step stools)
  • Criminal background check

Applications available at Circulation Desk of library.
